HELPFUL TOPICS

1. The item arrived damaged

No worries, you've got it all covered. All you need to do is:

  • send us some photos of the damaged item,
  • send us a video showing the item,
  • send us a photo of the warranty,
and we'll check everything for you. Independant of our work there is the shipping part in which we have to cooperate with other companies, so not everything in this process varies from us. After everything is checked we will refund you. Click here to email us or come visit us at a nearby store. Our main unit is located at:

2. The item is not as described in the photo

It is sometimes the case where we by mistake put another model or another product in the wrong shipping trip, however you don't need to worry. Since our shipment is very fast (up to 4-5 days), you need to send back the item and we will immediately ship another one, and as a reward for you patience we will award you with some percentage of the price according to this table:

Price range Off price

700+ € 8%

3. The item does not work

Before doing anything please check the following...
Our instructions:

  • you have followed all the instructions of the unboxing of the product,
  • you have read the manual provided by us,
  • you have received the right package.
Manufacturer's instructions
  • you have the read the manual,
  • you have checked if anything is missing in the box,
  • there wasn't any electrical fault when you first tried the item.
If after you have read all of the above you have not found the answer, then we are sorry for the inconvinience and please proceed with the same steps as in question 2.

Well yes, yes you can! All you need to do is provide us with the right information of what your merch will look like. You can do that by sending us all the info through email, the simplest form being creating a photo of your merch in a photo editing app like Adobe Photoshop.
